Mouse: Left = Rotate X/Y, Middle = Rotate Z, Right = Pan X/Y, Scroll = Pan Z

Object Controls Control he rotation of objects by selecting its radio-button, then dragging with the mouse.

Boat Controls: While selecting the radio button for the boat, the space-bar will pause the boat's revolution-cycle and its rotation, while the plus and minus keys will increase and decrease its speed.

Lighting Position Controls: At any time, pressing the keys "1," "2," or "3," will switch the position and rotation of the directional light between three different cases.
